Cookie Policy

How Elite Air uses cookies and similar technologies to keep our site working, measure performance, and improve your experience.

Last updated: 15 Aug 2025

Your consent to cookies

When visiting our websites for the first time, you will be asked for your consent to our use of cookies during your visit. You can change your choice at any time via the cookie banner (where available) or through your browser settings.

General information

Cookies are small data files that enable a website to collect and store data on your desktop computer, laptop, or mobile device. They help us provide important features and functionalities on our websites and mobile apps. Cookies are used to improve your experience as a guest. Please note that some features may not work correctly without certain cookies.

For example, cookies help us track which page you visit as you move through each stage of the ticket booking process.

Website analytics (for example, Google Analytics) provide statistical information about the number of visitors to our website and mobile apps, as well as which areas are most popular. Analytics also allow us to draw conclusions on trends — such as a page that attracts visitors from a particular country — so we can improve a visitor’s experience.

You can find general information about cookies and their functionality at the following websites:

  • www.allaboutcookies.org
  • www.youronlinechoices.eu

Cookie categories we use

Necessary cookies

Necessary cookies are required so you can browse our website and use its core functions (such as the booking engine and secure login). These cookies cannot be disabled in our systems.

Analysis cookies

Analysis cookies are used by us for the following purposes:

  • Monitoring traffic on the website and optimizing user experience.
  • Analyzing aggregated data about website usage to better understand customers.
  • Using random identifiers that may be shared with trusted partners for measurement.
  • Improving the way our websites and mobile apps work.

This Cookie Policy is dated 15 August 2025 and may be updated from time to time.
