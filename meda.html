MEDA — Medical Assistance

If you require medical clearance or special assistance to travel with Elite Air, please complete our MEDIF (Medical Information) form and send it to the MEDA team. This helps us prepare the right support for you onboard and on the ground.

Download MEDIF (PDF) Email the completed form to meda@eliteair.com.kw at least 4 days before departure.

Types of medical assistance

Wheelchair assistance

We provide WCHR/WCHS/WCHC support from curb to seat as required. Tell us your preferred assistance level on the MEDIF.

Portable oxygen concentrators

Approved POCs may be used onboard. Include device model and batteries on your MEDIF so we can verify compliance.

Medical clearance

Certain conditions, recent surgeries, or special equipment may require medical clearance. Our clinicians review MEDIFs confidentially.
